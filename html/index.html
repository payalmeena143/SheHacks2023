OUR POPULAR BRANDS

DOODLAGE

A brand whose sole mission is to turn waste into fashion, Doodlage uses deadstock fabric to create timeless pieces that are bound to change the way you perceive ‘patchwork’ forever.

Read More

Daughter Earth

This brand promises beauty without compromise through their all-natural, non-toxic, and plant-based cosmetics. While the products are high performance, we love that a portion of the brand’s profits is diverted towards conservation initiatives and the education of young girls.

Read More

The Moms Co.

Their natural mosquito repellent does not contain any mineral oil, petroleum or petroleum-derived ingredients, using instead plant extracts and essential oils.The resultant product is safe enough to be used on infants as young as five months old.

Read More

NO NASTIES

Just as its namesake signifies, No Nasties is a fair trade, organic, and vegan clothing brand whose key focus is creating planet positive clothing that offsets the carbon footprint of every product they make

Read More

Neeman’s

Neeman’s offers affordable and sustainable footwear alternatives that feel good, look good, and are great for the environment. Their footwear is composed of all-natural and renewable yarns including Merino wool and cotton, as well as alternatives made of recycled PET bottles.

Read More

11.11/ ELEVEN ELEVEN

A slow fashion brand that’s every minimalist’s dream aesthetic, 11.11 clothing is rooted in timelessness and offers a wide range of responsibly made pieces that promise longevity and versatility.

Read More

NICOBAR

Nicobar is a mindful brand that creates timeless clothing made from organic cotton, tencel, recycled wool and cupro modal. The brand collaborates with small communities across the country to develop their Merino wool scarves and works with fair trade factory partners to manufacture their clothing.

Read More

Paaduk

Shoe manufacturing can be very wasteful if resources are not used to their full potential. Paaduk stumbled upon a unique idea: to take a modern and eco-friendly step towards a better future by creating stylish and comfortable footwear out of old discarded tires
